Calibrated N15weather station data from pywws.

Each file has data every 5 minutes

comma (,) separates the data columns

data format

Column	data
1	idx		WANT THIS
2	delay
3	hum_in
4	temp_in
5	hum_out
6	temp_out	WANT THIS
7	abs_pressure
8	rel_pressure	WANT THIS
9	wind_ave
10	wind_gust
11	wind_dir
12	rain
13	status
14	illuminance
15	uv

So when plotting in gnuplot
x axis = column 1
y axis = column 6 for temperature
y axis = column 8 for pressure
